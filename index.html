AWS Student Community Day

"The Academic Advocacy of Bridging IT Industry Gap"

Join us for an insightful event on cloud computing, networking, and innovation.


Date: April 17, 2024 @ (8am - 5pm)
Location: PUP Bulwagang Balagtas

About the Event

The PUP College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS) and the PUP College of Engineering (COE) proudly presents the first Amazon Web Services (AWS) Student Community Day in the Philippines on April 17, 2024 at Bulwagang Balagtas. This groundbreaking event, designed for all tech and cloud enthusiasts, ensures participants gain valuable skills and knowledge about AWS services like data management, serverless architecture, cloud computing, and more! It aims to bridge the gap between industry demands and academic learning by emphasizing practical uses of AWS technologies. Under the theme "The Academic Advocacy of Bridging IT Industry Gap," attendees will explore creative AWS solutions while building community and mentorship.


    This event is:

  • FREE and OPEN TO ALL!

AWS Student Community Day Speakers

Dr. Werner Vogels

Vice President and Chief Technology Officer, Amazon

He is a Dutch computer scientist who is best known for his work in cloud computing and is a key figure in the development of Amazon Web Services (AWS), which is one of the largest cloud computing platforms in the world. Vogels is renowned for his expertise in distributed systems, scalability, and reliability, and he frequently shares his insights through speeches, writings, and public appearances in the tech community.

Mr. Raphael Quisumbing

Head of Product - Broadcast MediaTrack
AWS User Group Philippines Community Hero

As an IT Consultant, he provides businesses with cost effective technology with heavy emphasis on proper Data Collection and Information Process.

Mr. Paul Sears

Senior Solutions Architect, AWS Philippines

Paul has been in the Information, Communication Technology (ICT) and Operational Technology (OT) domains for more than 30 years in various leadership roles with various Enterprises on Systems Engineering, Data Center and Network Operations, DevOps, GenAI, Information Security, and Solutions Architecture. He engages with CXOs, Operations, Corporate Strategy and IT of customers and partners with portfolios of end-to-end Digital and Enterprise Solutions and Technologies covering mid-to-large scale business applications, operations and infrastructure across various areas.

Mr. Raphael Jambalos

Manager, ECloudvalley Philippines
AWS User Group Philippines Community Leader

He is a full-stack web developer, with a strong focus on backend development and managing cloud infrastructure. On his free time, he writes about developing web applications and getting them up and running for the cloud. He is also part of the leadership committee of the AWS User Group - Mega Manila and he's in charge of organizing the monthly meetups.

Mr. Mark Flores

Co-Founder and Co-Captain, AWS Cloud Club - PUP Manila

He is an undergraduate in the Polytechnic University of the Philippines taking a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, and an intern for Tutorials Dojo.
